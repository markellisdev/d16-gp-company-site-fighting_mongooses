Company Culture

Mongoose Artisan Fabrications is the result of Mr. Mongoosé's (pronounced Mongoo-sey) vision and desire to provide an outlet for mongoose creativity. Growing up in the deserts of Africa in colonies of up to 50 members, Mr. Mongoosé noticed that many young mongooses struggled emotionally. Most spent their time fighting with one another, hunting rodents, or battling poisonous snakes. One day as Mr. Mongoosé traveled out for a hunt, he trod through freshly crushed pomegranate. As he turned and reflected upon his journey, he noticed that his paw prints left behind a beautifully colored trail.
From that moment, he knew he had to help share art and creativity with the world. He created many works of his own and is credited with being the first mongoose artist. Many other pups followed along in his footsteps, pardon the pun, and he truly enjoyed helping them display and sell their works.

The Inspiration Station is a haven for mongoose artists and a place that inspires their natural curiosity and creativity. Hunting and fighting is not permitted in The Inspiration Station to enable each mongoose to focus in their own unique way without distraction. In the wild, mongooses typically reside in colonies of up to 50, but here, many prefer to work in solitude, although some do enjoy collaborating.
Materials for many different mediums are available and the natural beauty provides the perfect backdrop for creating true works of art.
